## Module 3 – Process for Commercial Projects

1. **Submit Property Details** – Use the form to begin.
2. **Site Walk** – Review structures, access and staging with your PM/facilities contact.
3. **Proposal** – Includes scope, timing, phasing and coatings.
4. **Scheduling** – Coordinate around operations as needed.
5. **Execution** – Crews handle cleaning, prep and refinishing.
6. **Close-Out Notes** – Optional photos and notes for maintenance records.
