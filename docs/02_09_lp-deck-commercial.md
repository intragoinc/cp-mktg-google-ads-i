## Section 3 – Process for Commercial Work

1. **Request Estimate** – Submit property details and photos if available.  
2. **Site Walk** – We review areas, access, staging and tenant impact.  
3. **Proposal** – Scope, surfaces, safety notes and scheduling plan.  
4. **Scheduling** – We coordinate access, staging and communication.  
5. **Execution** – Crews perform cleaning, prep, refinishing and safety cone-off as needed.  
6. **Close-Out** – Quick walkthrough, photos and notes for your records.
